Chapter 01 — The Paradigm Shift

Not an add-on.
The foundation.

Current perception: behavioral health platforms add layers — more data entry, more staff burden, more operational drag. But this misses the point entirely.

Vasl/VLAP isn't a tool clinicians use in addition to care; it's the infrastructure that makes evidence-based care possible.
