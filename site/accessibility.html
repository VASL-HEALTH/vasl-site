Vasl Health is committed to making our platform and public site usable by everyone, including people who rely on assistive technology. This statement describes that commitment and how to reach us if something isn't working for you.

1. Our Commitment

Vasl Health is committed to WCAG 2.1 AA accessibility standards across all platform properties — the public site, the member, coach, and clinician portals, and organizational dashboards. Accessibility is treated as an ongoing responsibility, not a one-time audit, and is factored into design and engineering decisions as the platform evolves.

2. Standards We Follow

We design and build against the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1, Level AA, published by the World Wide Web Consortium (W3C). These guidelines cover areas including:

  • Keyboard navigability for all interactive elements
  • Sufficient color contrast between text and background
  • Descriptive text alternatives for non-text content
  • Predictable, consistent navigation and page structure
  • Compatibility with screen readers and other assistive technology

3. Ongoing Work

Known Limitations

No platform is perfectly accessible at every moment. Some pages and components are still being brought fully in line with our WCAG 2.1 AA target. If you encounter a barrier, please tell us — reports directly shape what we prioritize next.

We review accessibility as part of our regular design and development process and welcome outside feedback as a way of catching issues our internal review misses.

4. Feedback & Contact

If you experience any difficulty accessing content or using any part of the Vasl Health platform or website, please contact us at accessibility@vaslhealth.com. Please include the page or feature involved and, if possible, the assistive technology and browser you were using — this helps us reproduce and fix the issue faster.
