2. Description of Services

Vasl Health provides a culturally-responsive emotional health platform that includes:

  • Member App providing peer community, daily check-ins, culturally matched coaching, and crisis resources
  • Vasl Language Analysis Platform (VLAP) — a proprietary AI clinical decision-support tool that processes member language in-memory to generate dimensional signal profiles for licensed clinicians and certified coaches
  • Clinical outcome tracking and population-level reporting for organizational administrators
  • Crisis detection and human clinical escalation protocol with 90-minute licensed supervisor response SLA
  • Organizational portals for coach management, clinical coordination, and compliance documentation

Our Services are designed to supplement, not replace, professional mental health care and are used in conjunction with appropriate clinical oversight. VLAP is a clinical decision-support tool — it is not a diagnostic system and does not replace clinical judgment.
