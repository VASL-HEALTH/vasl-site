How long does implementation take?

Six weeks from contract signature to first member enrollment — consistently, across org types. Week 1–2: scoping and configuration. Week 3–4: staff orientation and clinical briefing. Week 5–6: member enrollment opens with your implementation lead available for real-time support. Your organization doesn't need a dedicated technology team to deploy.

What does pricing look like?

Two components: a platform fee and a per member per month (PMPM) fee. Starter is $480/month platform fee for up to 100 members. Pro is $1,200/month for up to 500 members. PMPM is $3–5 for schools and non-profits, $6–8 for health systems and enterprise. Enterprise is scoped based on deployment size and requirements. See the full pricing page for a complete breakdown.

What compliance standards does Vasl meet?

HIPAA technical safeguards — full implementation across all platform components. BAA required for all organizational deployments and included in every contract. FERPA-aligned architecture for school deployments — student health data is structurally inaccessible to school administrators. SOC 2 Type II — annual third-party audit. Full compliance documentation is available under NDA for institutional review.

Can we do a pilot before committing to a full deployment?

Yes — and we recommend it. The scoping conversation helps us design a pilot that's large enough to produce meaningful outcome data but small enough to manage as a proof of concept. We'll discuss pilot structure, timeline, and success metrics during your demo.

Can Vasl help with grant funding?

We provide grant documentation — IRB study data, pilot outcome reports, VLAP technical overview, and compliance documentation — in formats designed for grant applications. For school districts, we also support Medicaid billing setup and Title IV-A documentation. We don't write grants on your behalf, but we make sure you have the outcome evidence grant funders need to see.

What happens after I submit this form?

You'll receive an acknowledgment email immediately. An implementation lead — not a sales representative — will follow up within one business day to schedule your demo at a time that works. We'll review what you submitted before the call so we come prepared with context specific to your org type and population.
